🤝 Sharing with your wife
  1. Create a folder in Google Drive (e.g. Kitchen Books)
  2. Right-click → Share → add your wife's Google account
  3. Both of you open the app → go to Settings → choose the same folder
  4. The app automatically reads and writes the same kitchen-data.json
